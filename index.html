Mission: To connect, create, and cultivate theatre in South Carolina.

Vision: To transform the lives of all South Carolinians through theatre.

Purpose: The purpose of the South Carolina Theatre Association is to bring together theatre groups – Educational Theatre, Community Theatre, Professional Theatre, and High School Theatre – to improve and further the cultural growth of theatrical art by mutual aid and advancement and stimulation of public interest.
